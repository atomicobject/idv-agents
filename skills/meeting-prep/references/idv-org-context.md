## Specific Funds & Programs — Distinguish These Three

This is the most-likely-to-be-confused area in IDV-mode output. Get this right or lose credibility with any IDV partner instantly.

### 1. ID Ventures direct funds [VERIFIED but check size not publicly disclosed]
- IDV's own multi-fund seed-stage venture capital
- idventures.com references *"multiple funds"* but specific fund names, sizes, and check ranges are **not publicly disclosed**
- Only public anchor: $50K example for Alerje (likely not representative)
- [FLAG] Typical check size range — confirm with IDV before citing as fact

### 2. Michigan Innovation Fund (MIF) [VERIFIED — important context]
- **$60M state program** signed by Gov. Whitmer on January 13, 2025 (House Bills 5651, 5652, 5653)
- **NOT an IDV-owned fund** — it's a state-level evergreen-fund support program
- Distribution:
  - $48M into 5 existing evergreen funds
  - $4.8M into emerging evergreen funds
  - $7.2M for ecosystem support and pitch competitions
- **ID Ventures receives $10.6M** from MIF (one of 5 existing recipients)
- Other recipients: Ann Arbor SPARK, U-M Accelerate Blue Fund, MSU Research Foundation, WMU Biosciences Research and Commercialization Center
- *Evergreen fund* = no fixed end date; reinvests proceeds from exits into new investments

**Skill usage — DO NOT** imply IDV manages the $60M. IDV is a $10.6M recipient, not the manager. *"Powered by MIF support"* is accurate; *"IDV's $60M MIF fund"* is wrong.

### 3. Venture 313 [VERIFIED]
- **$10M Gilbert Family Foundation program**, launched September 2022
- **NOT an IDV-owned fund** — Invest Detroit is a *founding partner* alongside TechTown Detroit and Detroit Development Fund
- 3-year commitment for Detroit-based founders
- Check sizes: **$500–$250,000** (mix of grants and equity-like investments via partners)
- Detroit Development Fund piece: $25K–$250K loans, lines of credit, working capital
- Focus: Detroit-based founders, minority/underrepresented founders, those *"piloting new models"* or *"may not otherwise fit the mold for traditional venture capital"*

**Skill usage**: Detroit-based founders may fit Venture 313 as a complement to (or instead of) ID Ventures direct. Surface this branch when the founder mentions Detroit residency. Out-of-Detroit founders do NOT fit Venture 313 — don't suggest it.

### FAM Program [FLAG — needs verification]
- Mentioned in internal IDV-context summary as *"Focuses on supporting underrepresented founders"*
- **Not surfaced in public web search**. May be IDV-internal naming or under-publicized.

**Skill usage**: When a founder asks about FAM, mark `[FLAG: FAM Program details not publicly disclosed; ask Belvin Liles or your IDV contact]`. Don't invent structure, check size, or eligibility.

### Strategic Neighborhood Fund (SNF) — NOT VENTURE

- **Out of scope for this skill.** If a founder mentions SNF, redirect: *"SNF is Invest Detroit's real estate program, not the venture arm. For venture, you want ID Ventures."*
